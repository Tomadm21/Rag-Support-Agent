## Refund Policy

### Eligibility
- Refunds can be requested within 30 days of purchase
- Only for first-time subscriptions
- Must provide reason for refund

### Process
1. Navigate to Settings > Billing > Transaction History
2. Click "Request Refund" next to the charge
3. Fill out the refund form with reason
4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

### Exceptions
- No refunds for annual subscriptions after 30 days
- No refunds for additional user seats
- No refunds for overage charges

## Duplicate Charges

If you see duplicate charges:
1. Check your transaction history first
2. Sometimes charges appear as "pending" and then "complete"
3. If truly duplicated, contact billing@support.com
4. Provide: transaction ID, date, and amount
5. Duplicates are refunded within 3-5 business days

## Upgrading & Downgrading

### Upgrading
- Instant upgrade - charges prorated amount immediately
- New features available within minutes
- No downtime during upgrade

### Downgrading
- Takes effect at end of current billing cycle
- You keep current features until cycle ends
- Data is retained for 90 days

## Payment Failures

### Common Causes
- Expired credit card
- Insufficient funds
- Card declined by bank
- Billing address mismatch

### Resolution
1. Update payment method in Settings > Billing
2. Retry failed payment
3. If issues persist, contact your bank
4. You have 7 days grace period before account suspension

## Invoices & Receipts

- Automatic email receipts after each charge
- Download invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoices
- Invoices include itemized breakdown
- Can customize invoice details (company name, tax ID, etc.)

## Tax & VAT
